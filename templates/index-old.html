EDUVERSE is an effort to bring closer the long awaited wish of human race to live in The Virtual Reality.
We are on the edge of creating a world that can help billions of people to participate, create, meet and explore things and places they have forever dreamt of.
EDUVERSE can be accessed using any VR enabler device on any platform. People who can not work with VR will also be able to join with their favorite Operating Systems as the EDUVERSE will be available on all platforms.
